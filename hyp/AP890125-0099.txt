Leopold Gratz the president of Parliament and one of the most powerful politicians in the Socialist Party resigned today over a scandal surrounding the mysterious sinking of ship 12 years ago
Gratz 59 is the second senior Socialist official to step down because of his alleged involvement in the so-called ``Lucona'' scandal the subject of a widely publicized investigation by a parliamentary committee
Interior Minister Karl Blecha like Gratz a onetime protege of former Chancellor Bruno Kreisky resigned Jan19
