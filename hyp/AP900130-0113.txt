The San Francisco 49ers' 55-10 rout of the Denver Broncos was the lowest-rated Super Bowl in 21 years and the third-lowest ever
The game on CBS averaged a 390 rating and a 63 share the lowest Super Bowl rating since 1969 when the New York Jets' 16-7 victory over Baltimore got a 360 on NBC for the worst rating ever AC Nielsen Co said today
Despite the low rating Sunday's game was seen by about 1085 million people making it the ninth most-watched TV show ever in the United States behind eight Super Bowls and the final episode of ``MASH''
Last year's San Francisco-Cincinnati Super Bowl won by the 49ers 20-16 got a 435 rating and was seen in 393 million homes according to Nielsen estimates
``Life Goes On'' on NBC got a 60 rating and ``Love With a Twist'' on NBC a 76
