President Bush congratulated the San Francisco 49ers on Friday and said they ``rewrote the record books'' with their Super Bowl victory
In turn he was presented with two autographed balls and a jogging outfit
``Let me congratulate you also on your victory'' quarterback Joe Montana told Bush at a East Room reception
Montana said being invited to the White House ``means a lot to all of us'' and joked ``Not very often do I get to see these guys in ties''
Bush was presented autographed balls by both Montana and wide receiver Jerry Rice who won the game's Most Valuable Player award
