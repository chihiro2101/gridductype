Thousands of John Lennon fans some too young to remember the Beatles swayed and sang along with songs including his anthem ``Imagine'' at a hometown tribute
Lennon's widow Yoko Ono asked the crowd
At the close of the concert she and son Sean 14 wept and hugged some of the musicians who included BB King Joe Cocker Roberta Flack the Moody Blues Hall and Oates Wet Wet Wet and Randy Travis
The concert was televised in Britain and was to be rebroadcast worldwide in October in a 50th birthday tribute to Lennon a decade after his death
Cyndi Lauper in a white minidress sang ``Hey Bulldog'' and Dave Edmunds backed by the Royal Liverpool Philharmonic Orchestra did the Beatles classic ``A Day in the Life''
On tape Starr performed ``I Call Your Name'' and said ``John had the biggest heart of any man I have ever met to this day
Lennon's co-songwriter Paul McCartney said on his tape ``Hello Liverpool scene of many great memories John me and the rest of the lads''
The concert closed with a recording of Lennon singing ``Imagine'' while giant screens showed a video featuring John and Yoko
