Damage from Hurricane Hugo which stormed through the Carolinas and the Caribbean last week will reduce third-quarter earnings for several insurers
New York-based American International Group Inc expects net losses related to Hugo to reduce pretax earnings between $35 million and $45 million
AIG insured property in Puerto Rico the eastern Caribbean Islands and the coastal regions of the Carolinas
St Paul Cos based in St Paul Minn said the pretax cost of Hugo shouldn't exceed $50 million
