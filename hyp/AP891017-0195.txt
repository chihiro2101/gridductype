A major earthquake rocked northern California Tuesday evening collapsing part of the San Francisco Bay Bridge and shaking Candlestick Park and buildings up to 95 miles away
Some buildings may have collapsed said Police Officer D Collins
There were reports of injuries at Candlestick where fans were waiting for the World Series to begin and the stadium was evacuated ABC-TV and Cable News Network reported
The quake which lasted about 15 seconds measured 65 on the Richter scale according to seismologists at the California Institute of Technology in Pasadena
The preliminary reading was from the Alaska Tsunami warning system Caltech said
