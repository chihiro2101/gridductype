Conductor Leonard Bernstein has canceled several performances in Japan due to exhaustion and lingering illness and will return to the United States to recover his manager announced today
``Leonard Bernstein is suffering from exhaustion as a result of trying to fulfill his professional obligations while not having fully recovered from several ailments he suffered this spring
Complete rest for an extended period is required if this remarkable man is to regain his usual high energy level'' said Dr Kevin Cahill the conductor's personal physician
Dr Angelo Aquista an associate of Cahill flew to Japan to accompany the conductor back to New York it said
