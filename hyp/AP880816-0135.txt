More than 4000 workers at a coal mine in the southern city of Jastrzebie went on strike today to demand legalization of Solidarity and higher wages a spokeswoman said
The strike in Jastrzebie about 275 miles southwest of Warsaw comes on the eighth anniversary of the founding of the outlawed trade federation
Solidarity spokeswoman Katarzyna Ketrzynska said workers on the overnight shift at the Manifest Lipcowy mine stayed outside the mine shaft all night and were joined by workers arriving for the morning shift
Workers at the Rudna copper mine near the city of Wroclaw staged a protest rally Monday to demand improved safety conditions and the return of Solidarity activists to work
